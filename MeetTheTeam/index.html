Our History

Travel Through Time With Us!

  • Inspiration!


    I was part of a group of high school students who were given a challenge to build a Minimum Viable Product and demo it to the Intuit employees. We applied lean-startup principles to build a rapid prototype and successfully demo a functional version during Intuit Incubation Week.

  • Working during our Junior Year.


    Though we had a lot of homework during our junior year, we managed to spend time on this project and deliver. We successfully built the backend using Parse.com and incorporated a login system.

  • Summer of Senior Year

    We worked with a team abroad to complete the project. We successfully configured the clock with a complex algorithm to calculate the time waste. We also developed a dynamic graph in which the graph will update automatically using AJAX.
